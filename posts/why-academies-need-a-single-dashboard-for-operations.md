### Having one dashboard for running the academy has its pros.

• Everyone, including students, staff, and courses, can access everything from one place.
• Easier scheduling and reminders that happen automatically
• Keeping track of students' performance and attendance in real time• Communication channels that work for both teachers and students

### Easier management of courses and batches

One of the best things about using a [Learning Management Tool](https://strawket.com/academies) is that it makes managing courses and batches easier. Academies can easily make and assign courses, keep track of who is enrolled, and plan sessions. Some common features are:

• Making schedules automatically

• Notifications for each batch

• Simple course changes that don't mess up schedules

• Systems for grading and assessing that work together

A centralized dashboard makes it easy for teachers to keep track of multiple courses and for students to see how they're doing, what assignments they have, and what feedback they have—all from one place.

### Analytics and making decisions based on data

A unified dashboard gives you in-depth analytics that can help you make smart choices. Academy management software typically tracks:

• Attendance and participation of students

• Rates of course completion

• Measuring how well instructors do

• Reports on money and collecting fees

With this information, administrators can find gaps, make the best use of course offerings, and make the whole operation run more smoothly. At strawket, we make dashboards that give academies useful information that helps them grow in a way that is good for the environment while still keeping high standards of education.

### Combining payment and administrative tasks

With a single dashboard, it's easy to keep track of fees, invoices, and reimbursements. Some common features are:

• Collecting and keeping track of fees online

• Automated reminders for payments that are due

• Writing about money transactions

• Managing payroll for staff

Academies save time, make fewer mistakes, and give students and staff a more professional experience by combining administrative and financial tasks into a Learning Management Tool.
