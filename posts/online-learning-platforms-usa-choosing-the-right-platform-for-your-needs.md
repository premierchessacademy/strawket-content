### Key Features to Look for in an Online Learning Platform

When evaluating online learning platforms, focus on features that enhance engagement and improve outcomes:

Interactive video lessons

Real-time assessments and quizzes

Progress tracking dashboards

Discussion forums or peer communities

Mobile accessibility and multi-device compatibility

Certifications or digital badges

A reliable platform should offer smooth navigation, responsive design, and minimal technical issues. Features such as live classes, one-on-one tutoring, and personalized learning paths add significant value for students who need additional support.
